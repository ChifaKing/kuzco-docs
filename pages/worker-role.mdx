# Worker role

You can earn specific Discord roles based on the points you accumulate, allowing you to unlock additional exclusive content and features within the server.

## Role Requirements

- **Bronze Worker Role**: 10M Points
- **Silver Worker Role**: 100M Points
- **Gold Worker Role**: 1B Points

## Steps to Obtain a Role


1. Earn the required number of points for the desired role.

2. Link your Discord account in the Kuzco [dashboard settings](https://kuzco.xyz/dashboard/settings).

3. Wait **2 hours** for automatic role assignment.

4. If the role is not assigned after **2 hours**, open a [Open a support ticket](https://discord.gg/kuzco).
