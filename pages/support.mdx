## How to use Kuzco Support

Kuzco Support is meant to cover as much ground as we can with common issues faced by the community during the Kuzco user journey. If it's your first time, here are the steps you should take to maximize Kuzco Support.

### Step 1: Verify if your issue is unique

Your are encouraged to go over <Link href="/support/general/faqs">General</Link>, 
<Link href="/support/dashboard/faqs">Dashboard</Link>, and <Link href="/support/worker/faqs">Worker</Link> to ensure that your issue isn't already addressed via Kuzco Support.

### Step 2. Open a support ticket on discord 

If your problem wasn\'t resolve when going through **Step 1** you may opt to [Submit an issue](https://github.com/context-labs/kuzco-docs/issues/new?title=Feedback%20for%20“FAQs”&labels=feedback) or [Open a support ticket](https://discord.gg/kuzco).
